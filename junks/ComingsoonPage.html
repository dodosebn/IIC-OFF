About Us

A UNESCO report highlights the immense potential of Africa's film industry, projecting annual revenue of $21 billion and the creation of over 20 million high-paying jobs.

However, this significant economic opportunity will remain largely unrealized unless African talent is adequately trained and equipped with the necessary skills. Africa is a continent brimming with creative individuals, but they require the right guidance to fully unleash their potential on both domestic and international stages.

Idea Is Capital presents professional Screen Acting and Screenwriting programs, delivered by experienced industry professionals. We believe in showcasing the incredible talent that exists within Africa. Don't let this $21 billion industry and its millions of jobs slip away. Keep 20 million high-paying jobs in Africa. The journey begins with Idea Is Capital. Join us today and help shape the future of African cinema.
